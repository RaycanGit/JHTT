Trading Model

Jinghaitai primarily engages in physical spot trading. Pricing mechanisms and delivery terms vary depending on market conditions and contractual structure.

For domestic transactions, delivery may be linked to exchange-recognized mechanisms where applicable. For international transactions, pricing is typically referenced to an agreed index over a defined averaging period prior to vessel loading, consistent with industry practice.
