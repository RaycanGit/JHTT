Operations and Delivery Model

Execution across domestic and international markets, structured for bulk trading workflows.

Operating Process

Structured execution
Sourcing and allocation
Sourcing and allocation from qualified suppliers based on contract requirements.
Delivery readiness
Preparation for storage and delivery through port and logistics infrastructure.
Inspection coordination
Coordination of inspection and supervision as required by contractual terms.
Logistics execution
Execution through ports or direct delivery to plants according to agreed terms.
Settlement discipline
Disciplined settlement supported by accounting and settlement functions.

Delivery Pathways

Domestic vs. International
Domestic market
Delivery may be executed through exchange-recognized mechanisms, including designated delivery warehouse arrangements where applicable.
International market
Delivery is typically executed on a physical shipment basis. Pricing is commonly referenced to an agreed index over a defined averaging period prior to vessel loading, in line with prevailing industry practice.
