Exchange-Recognized Delivery Capability

Designated Delivery Warehouse for Metallurgical Coke (DCE)

Institutional delivery capability within China’s formal commodity infrastructure.

Qualification

Jinghaitai is approved as a designated delivery warehouse for metallurgical coke at Qingdao Port (Dongjiakou Branch) by the Dalian Commodity Exchange (DCE).

Exchange-designated delivery warehouse signage (Qingdao Port, Dongjiakou Branch).

What This Means

An exchange-recognized designated delivery warehouse is an approved facility used for standardized delivery under futures contracts, subject to exchange supervision and operational requirements.

Why It Matters

This qualification reflects compliance with exchange-level operational and delivery standards and demonstrates structured delivery readiness within China’s formal commodity infrastructure.

It signals operational maturity, delivery discipline, and risk awareness for counterparties evaluating execution capability.

Practical Relevance

Domestic relevance
Supports standardized and transparent execution pathways where exchange-recognized delivery mechanisms apply.
International relevance
Signals disciplined operations and delivery credibility within the Chinese commodity market environment.

Additional recognition: Mysteel awards in 2021 and 2022.
